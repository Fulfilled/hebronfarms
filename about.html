We Love Farming

We are an agro-based solution company with interest in rice production, processing and marketing, poultry production and feed milling with a unified aim of leveraging on the on-going agricultural revolution in the country to carve a niche for ourselves in rice production, poultry production, and feed milling, thereby becoming a household name and renowned supplier of these agro products.

About Hebron

The Company

Hebron Group was registered with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in 2013. In the last six years, we have evolved into an integrated Agro business and services magnate operating in Nigeria with Africa as its focus. Our business approach is reflected in our slogan- “Structured Ingenuity”, which articulates our resolution and ability to deliver products and services based on tested methodology that reflects our ethos of overcoming challenges with innovative solutions.

The Vision

At Hebron, our vision is to develop and provide quality agricultural products and services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers. We work to build long term relationships with our out-growers and customers through production, processing, and marketing agricultural products through innovation and advanced technology.
