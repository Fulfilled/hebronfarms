Our Products

At Hebron, our focus is on the end product. We are committed to giving you value for your money and puting a smile of satisfaction on your face. That is why everything about us, from our policies, to operations, to careful sample analysis is to ensure we offer you the best product in the market. Below is a glossary of our available products.

Poultry

Our poultry farm is capable of producing over 20,000 tons of poultry meat and eggs per year and is expected to increase production to 30, 000 tons thereby earning you an all year round supply of your in-demand product – be it chicken or egg.

We employ top poultry specialists and farm maintenance team who work round the clock to ensure the birds have access to clean water and well-balanced ration feed that suits their production level. Furthermore, we employ routine medications and vaccinations that ensure the wellness of our birds to keep them safe from viruses, bacterial infections and pathogens.

Our chicken breed is obtained from reputable hatchery that maximizes your return on investments, whether you are buying our broilers or the eggs.

Feed

Our 110 metric tons per day of A-grade feed mill can sufficiently supply and sustain any viable livestock and poultry production enterprise. Hebron feed mills produces high quality feeds that generates high productivity, and eventually high return on investments for the poultry farmers.

In order to ensure quality of feeds for your livestock, Hebron feed mills are located away from environmentally-polluted areas and other potential contaminations, giving you a mix of top quality and hygienic feeds.

At Hebron, our poultry and livestock feeds are devoid of chemical, physical and biological hazards. All raw materials and finished products comply with sanitary and phytosanitary standards because the health of your livestock is our utmost concern.

We believe that if you gain, we gain too. Our feeds are used extensively and exclusively in our poultry farms, to assure you of the 100% trust we have in the quality our products.

Rice

We have rice farms in three states: Akwa Ibom, Benue and Nassarawa with a combined capacity to produce over 15000 tons of rice per year. We work with both foreign and local experts to evolve local rice into rice with superior quality, well-packaged and affordable with excellent nutritional standards.

Our state-of-the-art rice processing plants ensures nothing but quality – because at Hebron, quality is our watchword!

We don’t just stop at production and processing; our marketing strategy covers depots that are closer to the wholesaler, retailer and the consumer, with future plan of interstate delivery. Our products are transported directly from our farms to the warehouses and then to the points of supplies, thereby leaving no room for quality compromise.

We also buy rice from selected local rice farmers through the out-grower scheme to supplement our production because we believe in supporting the local rice farmers and growing the local rice industry. We are working to ensure that Hebron rice becomes a household name in the supply of this staple food.
